Hello, there! Thanks for visiting my site. My name is Erin Wilcox and I am the photographer behind FondPhotos.com. I love photography because life goes way too fast and sometimes I just want to press the pause button (or at least as close to a pause button as we can get). For me, photography is about capturing those special little moments with the people you love - creating an image that tells the story of a particular moment in time. Right now I'm a part-time stay-at-home mom and a part-time software developer, but I'd love to get more into my passion - photography. At the moment, I'm taking pictures completely for free to build up my portfolio. I'd love to do 1-2 hour photoshoot for you at no cost. I will provide 20ish edited pictures available for download. The only catch is that I ask that you allow me to use your pictures on this site. I am based out of Lehi, UT and am willing to shoot as far north as Salt Lake City and as far south as Provo. If you're interested in booking a photoshoot please text me at (801) 856-0302.
